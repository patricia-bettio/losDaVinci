Diana Stroie

Painter based in Versailles, France

Fullfilling the empty spaces of your home with paintings that inspire, provoke and awake true emotions.

Mysterious places such as the Bucegi mountains and imaginary places like Atlantis.
They don’t have anything in common, but I’m fascinated by the stories «we» as humans, have created since the beginning of humanity.
